How do I get there?

From Sarasota
The lab is located over the Ringling Bridge, on City Island. It's a few minutes past St. Armand's Circle. Click here for directions.

Once you're over the bridge, just follow the brown signs.

From the southeastern US
Sarasota is located one hour south of Tampa on I-75. But you probably already figured that out.
From the wild blue yonder
You can fly into either Tampa International Airport (TPA) or Sarasota-Bradenton International Airport (SRQ). SRQ is closer, but TPA often has beter rates.
