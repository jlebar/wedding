How do I get there?
Via GPS / Google Maps
Mote Marine Laboratory & Aquarium

From Sarasota
Mote Marine is located over the Ringling Bridge, on City Island. It's a few minutes past St. Armand's Circle. Click here for directions.

Once you're over the bridge, just follow the brown signs.

From the southeastern US
Sarasota is located one hour south of Tampa on I-75.
From elsewhere
You can fly into either Tampa International Airport (TPA) or Sarasota-Bradenton International Airport (SRQ). SRQ is closer, but TPA often has better rates.
