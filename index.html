From Sarasota: Getting to Mote Marine is easy! The lab is located on City Island, over the Ringling Bridge and past St. Armand's Circle. Click here for directions. Once you're over the bridge, just follow the signs.

From the southeastern US: Sarasota is located one hour south of Tampa on I-75. Driving here is fairly straightforward. Contact us if you need addresses to use in your GPS/map search engine of choice. See the link above for driving directions to the lab.

From the everywhere else: We recommend flying into Tampa International Airport (TPA) for the best rates. A closer but sometimes more expensive airport is the Sarasota-Bradenton International Airport (SRQ). If you plan to do a lot of sight-seeing while you're here, renting a car is probably your best choice once here. However, a free trolley service runs up and down the coastline, so if you stay by the beach, you probably don't need a car. More information on accommodations will be made available soon.
