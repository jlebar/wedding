Who/what/when/where?

Justin and Caroline are excited to announce their engagement! We will be getting married on August 13, 2011. Our ceremony will be at 1:00pm in the Keating Marine Education Center at Mote Marine Laboratory and Aquarium in Sarasota, FL.

We hope you will join us!

So it's inside an aquarium?

Actually, the Keating Education Center is a separate building from the main aquarium, and it doesn't house any marine life. The aquarium is just across the street, though, if you want to visit!

How do I get there?
Via GPS / Google Maps
Mote Marine Laboratory & Aquarium

From Sarasota
Mote Marine is located over the Ringling Bridge, on City Island. It's a few minutes past St. Armand's Circle. Click here for directions.

Once you're over the bridge, just follow the brown signs.

From the southeastern US
Sarasota is located one hour south of Tampa on I-75.
From elsewhere
You can fly into either Tampa International Airport (TPA) or Sarasota-Bradenton International Airport (SRQ). SRQ is closer, but TPA often has better rates.
